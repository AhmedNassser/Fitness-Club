BOXING

Looking to improve your endurance and build stamina?

Our intensive boxing classes will help you build muscle, lose weight and improve your hand-eye coordination. Get ring-ready with our boutique boxing class offerings that are included in your premium membership. Our boxing classes perfectly complement our fitness courses, including our Bootcamp, Altea 20-20-20 and Core Blaster – all you need to help you lose weight, feel great and look your best.

Following your intensive boxing workout, indulge in some much-needed hydrotherapy at our two hydrotherapy pools – designed to help ease weary and tired muscles, improve circulation, and decrease joint pain. If the thought of that doesn’t instantly make you say ahhh, try our AquaMoon Sensory Shower or KLAFS Sanarium and Steam Cabins.
